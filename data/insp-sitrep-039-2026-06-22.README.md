# INSP Ebola SitRep No. 39 — extracted data

Source: DR Congo Institut National de Santé Publique (INSP), "Rapport de Situation de la 17ème Epidémie de la Maladie à Virus EBOLA / RDC", SitRep N°39.
- Reporting date (date de rapportage / data cutoff): 22 June 2026
- Publication date (date de publication): 23 June 2026
- Original language: French. Field names and region names translated to English.
- Obtained via the INRB-UMIE/BDBV2026-Data mirror (file SitRep_MVE_039_2026.pdf); the primary site insp.cd was not directly reachable from the build environment.

## Files
There are two files for this SitRep, both derived from the same source PDF:
- `insp-sitrep-039-2026-06-22-by-province.csv` — province-level summary (Ituri, North Kivu, South Kivu, Total).
- `insp-sitrep-039-2026-06-22.csv` — the same data with an additional per-health-zone breakdown of confirmed cases/deaths.

The column explanations below apply to both files (the province-only file omits the `level` and `region` columns).

## Columns
- publication_date, data_cutoff_date: ISO dates from the report.
- level: Province | Health zone | Total.
- province, region: affected area. "Other zones not yet identified" corresponds to "Autres zones non encore identifiées" (confirmed cases not yet attributed to a specific health zone).
- cumulative_confirmed_cases ("Cas confirmés cumulés") and cumulative_confirmed_deaths ("Décès confirmés cumulés"): from Tableau 1 (province) and Tableau 2 (health zone).
- suspected_cases_in_isolation: from Tableau 6 ("Patients en isolement (Fin J) ... dont suspects"). This is an END-OF-DAY (22 June) point-in-time snapshot reported at PROVINCE level only — not a cumulative figure and not available per health zone.

## Fields marked N/A
SitRep No. 39 does NOT report the following metrics, so they are left as N/A rather than estimated:
- cumulative_suspected_cases: the report gives only DAILY suspects ("cas suspects du jour" = 131 total on 22 June, by province: Ituri 61, North Kivu 70, South Kivu 0), not a cumulative total.
- suspected_cases_under_investigation: not reported as a discrete figure (the report tracks "alertes investiguées" — investigated alerts — which is a different concept).
- cumulative_suspected_deaths: the report gives only DAILY suspected deaths (44 on 22 June), not a cumulative total.
- Per-health-zone suspect figures: not reported at all; suspect data exists only at province level.

## Verify before publishing
These figures are extracted from the INSP SitRep PDF and should be verified against WHO Disease Outbreak News before publication. Note the cover page marks the cumulative confirmed total (1,094) as "Données réactualisées" (data updated after DHIS2 cleaning/harmonisation).
