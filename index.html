Contributors, Data & Methods

Data source

All epidemiological figures are transcribed from the World Health Organization Disease Outbreak News:

WHO DON607 — Ebola disease caused by Bundibugyo virus, DRC & Uganda (13 June 2026)

DRC figures are as of 10 June 2026; Uganda figures as of 11 June 2026. WHO cites the Centre des opérations d'urgences de santé publique (COUSP-DRC) as the underlying DRC data source.

Method

  • The map is a proportional-symbol layer: each circle is an Ituri health zone with WHO-published confirmed case counts (Bunia 185, Rwampara 137, Mongbwalu 132, Nyankunde 33). Circle area is proportional to cases; color follows a sequential red ramp.
  • The log / linear toggle changes how case counts map onto the color ramp. The marker toggle shows or hides the case symbols.
  • North Kivu (9 affected health zones) and South Kivu (1) are shown as context markers — DON607 does not disaggregate their case counts. 94 DRC cases were awaiting health-zone distribution at the time of reporting.
  • Marker coordinates are approximate health-zone centroids for visual placement, not official boundaries.
  • The trend chart uses cumulative confirmed counts from successive WHO DONs (21 May, 8 June, 10 June).
